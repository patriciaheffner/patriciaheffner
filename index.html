Hi, I'm Patricia

Born in Detroit, raised in the suburbs in a small(ish) town. My immigrant parents arrived with little to their names, became naturalized citizens, and taught me the value of hard work and never giving up.

After graduating from Michigan State University where I got my bachelor of science in biology and met my husband, Patrick, I moved to DC. I have now been a 3 year resident of Alexandria, where it seems mandatory to have a dog (do not, but desperately would like to). I enjoy taking classes at the Art League - including watercolor, oil painting and jewelry making - walking around Old Town, and trying new restaurants with friends.

A constant in my life has been creating, from calligraphy, hand lettering, pottery, and painting. I also inherited the "tinkering" gene from my father - someone who explores to mechanically understand how things work. My life is at an interesting juncture, where my innate curiousity, creativity and methodical science degree progressed into my career of development.

Current

Currently holding down the fort as the only female developer (on a team of 6) building a React web app simulating (a pixel perfect browser version of) Microsoft Office Suite. Does it sound impossible? No way! We've been sprinting to the end (although, is there really ever an end to development?) My favorite challenges include rebuilding complex Excel functions, replicating various styles & formatting, and creatively approaching problems.

I'm also diving into a MEAN stack project, aspiring to submit an iOS app (one day, and it will be terrifying and exciting), and finally signing up for Skillshare to get even more creative.
